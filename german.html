German

From my German classes, I really would not be the same person today with learning so much about not only the German language but also so much of another culture. In German club, I explored various fun aspects of Germany such as creating Apfelpuppen (Apple Dolls) and experiencing Kaffee Klatsch, a traditional coffee gathering in which I had many conversations with friends.

In German class, I also got to learn about German culture and did many fun activities revolving around topics like Handball, cultural stories, renting an apartment, German train stations, and many more. I also gained insight into the cultural differences between Germany and the United States, such as the different gestures and lifestyles each culture has.
